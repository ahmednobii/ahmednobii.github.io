a:T87a,<h1>Hello World — Building My Portfolio with Next.js</h1>
<p>Welcome to my blog! I'm <strong>Ahmed Nobi</strong>, a Senior Android Engineer with over 7 years of experience building high-performance mobile applications. This is the first post on my new portfolio site, and I wanted to share a bit about why I built it and what's coming next.</p>
<h2>Why a Developer Portfolio?</h2>
<p>As engineers, we spend most of our time writing code that lives behind app stores and company firewalls. A portfolio gives me a space to:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Share knowledge</strong> from years of building fintech and media applications</li>
<li><strong>Document architectural decisions</strong> and trade-offs I've encountered</li>
<li><strong>Explore ideas</strong> around Kotlin Multiplatform, Jetpack Compose, and modern Android development</li>
</ul>
<h2>The Tech Stack</h2>
<p>This site is built with:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Next.js 14</strong> with App Router for static site generation</li>
<li><strong>Tailwind CSS</strong> for a clean, dark-mode design</li>
<li><strong>Markdown</strong> for blog posts (just add a <code>.md</code> file and deploy)</li>
<li><strong>GitHub Actions</strong> for automated deployment to GitHub Pages</li>
</ul>
<p>The entire site is statically exported, meaning it's fast, secure, and costs nothing to host.</p>
<h2>What's Next?</h2>
<p>I plan to write about topics I'm passionate about:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP)</strong> — sharing production experience, iOS interop challenges, and architectural patterns</li>
<li><strong>Jetpack Compose</strong> — performance optimization, custom layouts, and animation techniques</li>
<li><strong>System Design</strong> — mobile-first architecture, offline-first strategies, and scaling to millions of users</li>
<li><strong>Engineering Leadership</strong> — mentoring, technical decision-making, and building engineering culture</li>
</ul>
<p>Stay tuned, and feel free to connect with me on <a href="https://linkedin.com/in/ahmednobii">LinkedIn</a> or check out my work on <a href="https://github.com/ahmednobii">GitHub</a>.</p>
